Frequently Asked Questions

A typical detailing service can take anywhere from 2 to 8 hours, depending on the size of the vehicle and the level of detailing required. We'll provide you with a more accurate estimate when you book your appointment.

Our basic detailing package includes a thorough exterior wash, tire and wheel cleaning, interior vacuuming, surface cleaning, and window cleaning. We also offer more comprehensive packages that include additional services like waxing, polishing, and deep interior cleaning.

The longevity of ceramic coating depends on various factors, including the quality of the coating and how well the vehicle is maintained. On average, a professional ceramic coating can last anywhere from 2 to 5 years. With proper care and maintenance, some high-quality coatings can even last up to 7 years.
